Time 15 min
Servings 4

What You Will Need

Step One Start by first dicing the tomatoes, jalapeno, red onion. Mix these together. Dice the cilantro and juice the limes.
Step Two Next, peel the avocados, remove the seed, slice the avocados and place these on a plate.
Step Three Combine all your ingredients together, along with 1 tbsp of lime juice and 1/4 tsp of salt.
Step Four Taste the salad. If wanted, add more salt, lime juice, and cheese to taste!
Step Five Pick your favorite dressing (or the cheapest) and smother it!
