Barbecue Chicken

Time 20 min
Servings 2

what you will need

Step one Start by rinsing off the chicken under the sink and patting it dry.
Step two Next mix the onion and garlic powder with the tajin and oil into a bowl and coat the chicken in it. *make sure not to use all the oil*
Step three On a nonstick pan place heat to medium high with a dash of oil and wait till the oil is heated and place the chicken on for 4 minutes on each side.
Step four Take the chicken off of the skillet and brush with the desired amount of barbecue on both sides. Then let it cook for a minute longer.
Step five Now take off the skillet and you are ready to eat, best paired with rice and corn and/or bread!
