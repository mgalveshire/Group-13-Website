Mashed Potatoes

Time 25 min
Servings 10

what you will need

Step one Peel your potatoes and cut into evenly sized pieces. They should be around an inch thick, give or take.
Step two Boil your potatoes, starting in cold water, adding your garlic and a pinch of salt. Once boiling reduce the heat and cook for 8 to 10 minutes (Your potatoes should have little resistance when cut with a knife). Then drain water.
Step three Microwave butter, adding milk and another pinch of salt. Set this mixture aside.
Step four Wait until your potatoes have dried and begin to mash until you have reached your desired consistency. Pour over your butter mixture, half at a time, and fold until combined.
Step five Add salt to taste if more is needed and sprinkle on your toppings! Feel free to lightly heat up again before serving.
