Chips and Guacamole

Time 15 min
Servings 10

what you will need

Step one Start by first dicing the tomatos, jalapeno, red onion Mix these together. Dice the cilanto and juice the limes. Place these to the side
Step two Next peel the avacados remove the seed and place these in bowl. After this mash the avacdos with a fork
Step three Combine all your ingredients together. Along with 1 tbsp of lime juice and 1/4 tsp of salt
Step four Taste the guacamole. If wanted add more salt and lime. Most use up to 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tbsp lime juice
