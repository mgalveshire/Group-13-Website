Step one Start by first dicing the tomatoes, jalapeno, red onion. Mix together. Dice the cilantro and juice the limes. Place these to the side.
Step two Next, peel the avocados. Remove the seed and place these in bowl. Then, mash the avocados with a fork.
Step three Combine all your ingredients together. Along with 1 tbsp of lime juice and 1/4 tsp of salt.
Step four Taste the guacamole. If wanted add more salt and lime. Most use up to 1/2 tsp salt and 2 tbsp lime juice per batch.
