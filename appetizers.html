Chips and Guacamole

Time 15 min
Servings 10

    what you will need:

  • 2 tsp lime juice or 1 fresh lime
  • 2 roma tomatoes
  • 1 jalapeno
  • 1/4 red onion
  • 2 tbsp of cilantro
  • 3 medium avacados
  • 1/2 of salt
Step one
Start by first dicing the tomatos, jalapeno, red onion Mix these together. Dice the cilanto and juice the limes. Place these to the side
Step two
Next peel the avacdos remove the seed and place these in bowl. After this mash the avacdos with a fork
Step three
Combine all your ingredients together. Along with 1 tbsp of lime juice and 1/4 tsp of salt
